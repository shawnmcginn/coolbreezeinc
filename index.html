THE PROCESS

What To Expect

01

Unit Inspection

We start by inspecting your unit for signs of buildup or wear — making sure it's ready for a thorough clean.

02

Indoor Unit Clean

We carefully clean and sanitize the indoor head, including filters — restoring proper airflow and efficiency.

03

Sanitizer Applied

Our specialized cleaning solution breaks down and removes grease, dirt, and mold for a deep, effective clean.

04

Final Inspection

Your unit is fully sanitized and checked. Better performance and fresher indoor air — guaranteed.

01

Vent Inspection

We assess the level of lint buildup and check for blockages that could affect your dryer's safety or performance.

02

Vent Cleaning

We disconnect the vent and use high-flow compressed air to thoroughly clear lint and debris from the interior.

03

Thorough Passes

Multiple passes with our tools ensure every section is fully cleared — no lint, no blockages, no shortcuts.

04

Final Airflow Check

We reassemble everything and confirm airflow is back to normal — safe, efficient, done right.

Start to finish: 60–90 minutes
